montecarlopdf

What Is It?

A python module to assist with the web-scraping of pdfs.

The aim is to identify the position and nature of the table lines on a pdf using a monte-carlo approach and to use the details of the table lines to group together the text like a human would.

How Might It Be Useful?

  • To determine how many of the rows are column-headers.
  • To check whether bold table lines inicate the presence of table headers.
  • To determine whether the lack of table lines is used to group together two column.
